# Privacy Policy for LocalLedger

**Last Updated: Mar 05, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describing the LocalLedger mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**We do not collect, transmit, distribute, or sell your personal or financial data.**

LocalLedger is designed as an "offline-first" application. All financial data, transactions, goals, and account balances you enter into the App are stored entirely locally on your specific mobile device. 

We do not have backend servers that collect your personal records, nor do we have access to any of the financial information you input into the App. 

### Third-Party Services
While we do not collect your data, the App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you or process payments:
*   **Google Play Services / Apple App Store**: For app distribution and managing in-app purchases or subscriptions.
*   **RevenueCat**: Used to manage our freemium subscription model and validate purchase status. RevenueCat does not see your ledger data, but tracks your subscription entitlement via an anonymous App User ID.

## 2. Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
Your data is stored securely on your device's local storage. Because the data resides solely on your device, **you are entirely responsible for the security of your device and the data contained within the App.** 

We strongly recommend utilizing your device's built-in security features (such as passcodes, biometric authentication, and device encryption) to protect your information.

### Backups
If you utilize cloud backup services provided by your device's operating system (e.g., Google Drive, iCloud), your App data may be included in those backups according to your device settings. These backups are governed by the privacy policies and security practices of Google or Apple. We do not control and cannot access these backups.
